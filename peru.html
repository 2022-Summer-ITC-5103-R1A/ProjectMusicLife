In Peru there are different types of musical genres for all tastes. Firstly, can mention the "huayno", which was born in the Peruvian highlands with Andean instruments; the "marinera", elegant and rhythmic music accompanied by a couple of dancers; the Peruvian waltz, which was born in the time of the Viceroyalty and arrived until today as Creole music; the "chicha", which is the incredible mixture of "huayno", psychedelic rock and "cumbia guarachera"; the "festejo", which is characterized by its Afro-Peruvian rhythms and the use of instruments such as the Peruvian cajón; and finally, The "tondero", which is a northern Creole genre.

Peru has a great variety of music! And the best thing is that each gender has a 100% Peruvian identity
