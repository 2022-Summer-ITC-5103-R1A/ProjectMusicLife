Discription Coastal Region

On the Peruvian coast, rhythms such as the waltz, the "marinera", the "tondero", the "festejo" and others are performed. This music uses instruments of clear Spanish descent, such as the guitar and castanets, however, it also uses Peruvian instruments such as the Peruvian "cajon" and the donkey jaw of clear Afro-Peruvian descent.
