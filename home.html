About MUSIC LIFE

MUSIC LIFE is an non-profit leisure website which promote music culture. The objection of MUSIC LIFE is to promote music culture worldwide. People can listen music and play games in our website. We would introduce different types of music from everywhere and would organize different music events with different parties.
